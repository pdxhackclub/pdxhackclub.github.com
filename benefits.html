Benefits

1. Free pizza, every time we ship a project! Each project earns us $5 for every hour we spent working on it, solely to be spent on pizza. (actual regulation of the grants)

2. You get to spend time learning how to build up your career, discussing it with your peers.

3. You are a member of the Hack Foundation, which is great for your resume! It's also great fun.

4. You make free projects that you get to take home, funded by donations and grants.

5. YSWS: If you make a specific project, the Hack Foundation will ship out something related to that project! Anything from game stickers, to game consoles, to laptops.

6. We aren't always working, and the work we do is always fun!

7. Hackathons and Game Jams: Just from being a member, you get to attend game jams and hackathons hosted by us and by the Hack Foundation, for free!

8. Jet Fund: When you ship a project, you get some money towards Hackathon flights, depending on the hours on the project.


If this all sounds good to you, feel free to send in an application!
