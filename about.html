About Us

We are a community of teens (13–17), who electrical engineer (build things with microcomputers) and program (design apps and programs).

Everything we make is open-source, meaning that we publish everything for others to use and develop.

We meet once a week on saturdays, for two hours, in Multnomah County Central Library. During these meetings, we discuss programming, engineering, and have a short pizza party!

There are currently 0 members! To help us out, the best way is to join.

You must be a teenager to join.

If you can't join, feel free to donate
